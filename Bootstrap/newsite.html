WELCOME TO SHARNBASVESHWAR COLLEGE OF SCIENCE, KALABURAGI

(Run Under the aegis of Sharanbasaveshwar Vidya Vardhaka Sangha) Affiliated to Gulbarga University, Kalaburagi & Accredited with CGPA of 2.98 on four point scale at B++ Grade & ISO Certified. Sharnbasveshwar College of Science was started in 1956. This institution is one of the earliest science Colleges in this Hyderabad-Karnataka region. Over the year it has become one of the prestigious institutions as their has been a consistent infrastructure development with both men and material aiming and imparting quality education. This institution is located on a hill-lock covering an area of about 4-acres. It has a picturesque landscape with bountiful greenery in its surroundings. A person with an inquisitive mind may start wondering how this reputed institute has been in a position to carry out this process of learning and teaching effectively, and always imbibing the cult of pursing excellence among the teachers and the taught.

SHARANABASAVESHWAR VIDYA VARDHAK SANGHA, KALABURAGI.

The Sharanabasaveshwara Vidya Vardhak Sangha, which runs more than seventy institutions, founded in 1903, works on the education philosophy propounded by the President of our Sangha, Poojya Dr. Sharnbaswappa Appa. The philosophy goes thus, " No religion is greater than service. Teaching and learning not only for the sake of livelihood, but also for the welfare of others, thinking that serving humanity is serving God, is Kayaka Dasoha philosophy of education". As the famous poet Robert Frost says, we indeed have "Many miles to go and many promises to keep" to a great extent our management has been treading this path relentlessly.
